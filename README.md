# 💫 About Me:
I’m a final-year Applied Computer Science student at HoGent, specializing in AI & Data Engineering. I’m currently completing my internship at CodeFever and FTRPRF, where I’m gaining hands-on experience in building data-driven solutions and interactive dashboards.

I enjoy transforming raw data into meaningful insights and visualizations, working end-to-end from data collection and processing to actionable storytelling. My curiosity, attention to detail, and problem-solving mindset drive me to explore new tools and techniques continuously.

Passionate about AI, machine learning, and data engineering, I’m always eager to learn and collaborate. Feel free to connect—I’m open to exchanging ideas, knowledge, and opportunities in the tech and data space.
